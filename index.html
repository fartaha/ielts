Daily Speaking with Friends

8.8.2023

  1. How long have you lived in your home?
    • I’ve been living in my current home for about five years now. I moved in back in 2018.
  2. Do you like your home? Why?
    • Absolutely, I really like my home. It’s cozy, and I’ve made it my own with personal touches. The location is great too, it’s close to my workplace and all the amenities I need.
  3. Do all ages live in your area?
    • Yes, my neighborhood is quite diverse in terms of age groups. There are families with young children, working professionals, and even retirees. It’s a nice mix I would say.
  4. What kind of music do you like?
    • I’m a big fan of indie rock and alternative music. I love discovering new bands and enjoying the unique sound they bring. It just resonates with me.
  5. Is there music you don’t like?
    • Well, I’m not really into heavy metal or hardcore rap. They’re just not my cup of tea. But hey, to each their own, right?

    to each their own: used to say that everyone likes different things: You actually like modern jazz, do you? Each to their own. I try not to judge people who like different things from me - each to their own. I’m not personally a fan of model trains, but each to their own.

  6. Do all your friends like music?
    • Most of my friends are into music, but everyone has their own preferences. Some are really passionate about it, while others just enjoy it casually.
  7. When you were a kid, what would you like to do?
    • When I was a kid, I used to love spending time outdoors, exploring the woods near my house. I would also spend hours drawing and making up stories. It was a simple but imaginative time.
